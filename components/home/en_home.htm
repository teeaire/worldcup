The Feast World Cup

What Is The Feast World Cup?

The Feast World Cup is a community-run tournament hosted by PvPaissa that puts PvP Teams from North America, Europe, and Japan head-to-head to face off with the best teams in each region! Ever since the Feast Regional Championships (FRC) hosted by Square Enix and the Final Fantasy XIV (FFXIV) team, the community has taken notice of the effort they are making to become an e-sport. To end off the 2nd FFXIV expansion with a bang, The Feast World Cup will be a large event that will commence between the Japan FRC and the 3rd FFXIV expansion (late April – May). Building off from the FRC, all registered teams will have a chance to compete for a spot in The Feast World Cup and win a custom trophy as well as a $2,000 USD + crowdfunded prize pool!

Registrations are open now and will be open until April 12th, 2019.

Tournament Format

The Data Centers that will be used for the tournament are: Aether for North America, Chaos for Europe and Mana for Japan.

The Feast World Cup – NA/EU/JP Regional Qualifiers (April 19, 2019 - May 4, 2019)
  • Group Stage (April 19, 2019 - May 3, 2019)
    • Teams will be randomly placed into Groups.
    • Best-of-3 Round Robin with all teams in your respective group.
    • Top teams from each group will qualify for the Playoff Stage.
    • Group Stage will only occur if more than 8 teams are registered.
    • Format will change based on number of teams registered.
  • Playoff Stage (May 4, 2019)
    • Top 8 teams from the Group Stage will be facing off based on seeding in Group Stage.
    • Top 4 teams will be seeded from #1-#4 and qualify for The Feast World Cup: Main Event.
    • Best-of-3 single-elimination bracket.
    • Date & Time:
      • JP – May 11, 2019 @ 8 PM JST
      • EU – May 4, 2019 @ 8 PM GMT
      • NA – May 4, 2019 @ 7 PM PST
The Feast World Cup: Main Event (May 17, 2019 – May 18, 2019; May 24, 2019 – May 25, 2019)
  • Group Stage (May 17, 2019 – May 18, 2019)
    • Top 4 teams from NA, EU and JP Regional Qualifiers (12 Teams total) will be seeded into 4 groups of 3 teams.
    • Best-of-3 Round Robin with all teams in your respective group.
    • Top 2 from each group (8 teams) will be seeded #1 and #2 based on win/loss record within your group and advance to The Feast World Cup: Main Event - Playoff Stage.
    • Date & Time:
      • Group 1 – May 17, 2019 @ TBD
      • Group 2 – May 18, 2019 @ TBD
  • Playoff Stage (May 24, 2019 – May 25, 2019)
    • Top 2 teams from each group (8 teams) in 'The Feast World Cup Main Event - Group Stage' will advance to The Feast World Cup Main Event - Playoff Stage and face off based on seeding.
    • Best-of-3 double elimination bracket, Grand Finals best-of-5.
    • Date & Time:
      • Winner’s Quarterfinals, Loser’s Playoffs, Winner’s Semifinals – May 24, 2019 @ TBD
      • Loser’s Quarterfinals, Loser’s Semifinals, Winner’s Finals, Loser’s Finals and Grand Finals – May 25, 2019 @ TBD
